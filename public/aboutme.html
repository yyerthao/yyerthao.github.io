In 2015, I graduated from Minnesota State University, Mankato, MN with a Bachelors of Arts in English Literature, emphasis on Creative Writing. My professional scope at the time was bringing awareness to the experiences of marginalized folks. I dedicated my time researching and finding local voices to highlight, however I decided to pursue this another way; one of my goals is to create a workshop for young POC girls to get into coding.

Right out of college, I took a hiatus and started my precious little family. We reside in the Greater Twin Cities of Minnesota and absolutely love the four seasons here. After becoming a mom, I've become more bold and daring to challenge myself to do greater things. So, here I am. Not in a million years would I have thought I'd get into the tech field, but I'm here and loving it.

Some hobbies of mine include: cooking lots of spicy foods, trying to perfect my home made pho broth, watching Mukbang, meditation, yoga, and 'thinking' about writing (currently have 5 years of ongoing writer's block and counting++). Oh and I also love making bad code puns, and bad dad jokes that no one laughs at.
