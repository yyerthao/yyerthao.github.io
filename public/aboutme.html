About Me

I obtained my Bachelor of Arts in English Literature and dreamt of pursuing higher education. When I graduated in 2015, I decided to spend more time with my little family.

Fast-forward to present day, I am a Full Stack Software Developer. It is definitely a huge career change from origin choice of profession, Creative Writer professor. However, I am loving the different, dynamic work environment coding provides. This field is ever-so evolving so I know I won't ever have a dull day.
