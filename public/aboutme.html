About Me

In 2015, I graduated from Minnesota State University, Mankato, MN with a Bachelors of Arts in English Literature. My professional scope at the time was bringing awareness to the experiences of marginalized folks. I dedicated my time researching and finding local voices to highlight, however I decided to pursue this another way; one of my goals is to create a workshop for young POC girls to get into coding.

Another one of my greatest passions is having writer's block (5 years and counting++).
