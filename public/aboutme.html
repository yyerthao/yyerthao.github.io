About Me

In 2015, I graduated from Minnesota State University, Mankato, MN with a Bachelors of Arts in English Literature. I had plans to pursue higher education in order to teach creative arts in prose and poetry. However, today, I am a Full Stack Software Developer. It is definitely a huge career change from origin choice of profession, but I recognize the different, dynamic work environment coding provides me great pleasure. This field is ever-so evolving so on the up side, there will never be a dull work day.

Outside of code, I love spending time with my wonderful boys. This summer, we enjoyed creating obstacle courses in our backyard, going on hikes, and renovating our home together. Being a mom is one of the greatest gifts I've had the privilege to receive.

Another one of my greatest passions is having writer's block (5 years and counting++).
