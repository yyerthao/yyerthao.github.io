About Me

Aside from being a Full Stack Software Developer, I am also a poet with ongoing writer's block (5 years and counting++). It is definitely a huge career change from origin choice of profession. However, I am loving the different, dynamic work environment coding provides me. This field is ever-so evolving so I know I won't ever have a dull day.
