Education

In 2015, I graduated from Minnesota State University, Mankato, MN with a Bachelors of Arts in English Literature, with an emphasis on Creative Writing. My professional scope at the time was bringing awareness to the experiences of marginalized folks. I dedicated my time researching and finding local voices to highlight, however I decided to pursue this another way which is why this static portfolio site exists.


Forward a few years after, I enrolled and graduated from Prime Digital Academy, an immersive full time five month program. Here I learned the PERN (Postgresl, Express, React, Node), how to collaborate with other developers, as well as other aspects of being a developer (learning to communicate, learning about bias and so much more). If interested, please check out Prime Digital Academy. I will 100% always recommend them as they've changed my life for the best.

Gaps

Right out of college, I took a hiatus and started a family. Through the past few years, I recognized how important it is to give back. I asked myself, "How can I ensure my children's lives will be okay?" Through time, I realized it's not about providing them a life of rich goods but being part of the change that needs to happen for THEIR environment. With this in mind, I found myself exploring new roles to find my place in giving back. I held a variety of roles from tutoring English, mentoring college students, being a school aged teacher, to pharmacy technician. I found myself struggling to enjoy a daily life in the roles I took on. And now I'm here for the ongoing learning. I think it's safe to say there is never a dull day in tech for me, everyday I'm learning something.
