I'm a sun loving Full Stack Software Engineer who's made the transition into the tech in order to be in a space where I will always be challenged, and can always grow. Personally, I find it absolutely crucial for everyone to always learn something new each day; there's just too much too learn and we're all so capable to always make this effort.

I am not only that above, but a superhero mom of two boys, rookie garden enthusiast, and a writer. Content-wise, my theme of my written works are dedicated around raising awareness on marginalized folks. One of my goals this year is to publish a collection of my works.

Mentally - I believe in the power of empathy, kindness, and giving.
